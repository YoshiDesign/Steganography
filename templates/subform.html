Tips

Files must be of type .png and < 8Mb

Encoding increases the size of your message by a factor of 4.

.jpeg and .jpg images may be encoded from the CLI

Copy your picture before using the CLI tool; images are overwritten.

Do not resize JPEG's after encoding your message or it may be destroyed.

Messages larger than your picture will not be decodeable, but they may still be encoded for novelty sake.

Text which is not UTF-8 plaintext will not encode properly and cannot be retrieved. (Simply copy/pasting from websites won't always work)

If you encode an image multiple times the old messages are overwritten. If the web interface returns an old message it is due to browser caching, which can be disabled.
